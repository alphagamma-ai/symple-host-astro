2. **When you've outgrown spreadsheets** — clear signals it's time to move on, and the real cost of waiting.
3. **Simplifying operations with AI automations** — practical ways to reduce manual work across guest messaging, tasks, and daily ops.
4. **Scaling markets, branding, and marketing** — how strong operators grow beyond day-to-day firefighting.

6. **Live Q&A** — bring your real-world questions to operators and platform builders who've helped villas of every size make the switch.

## FAQ

### Is this really free?

Yes — 100% free. Million Dollar Host and Symplehost.ai are running this as a community learning session for the villa and short-term rental industry.

### What if I can't attend live?

Register anyway — we'll send you the recording and the PMS selection checklist within 48 hours after the event.

### Is this a sales pitch for Symplehost.ai?

No. We'll show what a PMS looks like using Symplehost as an example, but the framework is vendor-neutral. You'll leave with a checklist you can use to evaluate any system.

### Do I need to be a Million Dollar Host member?

Not at all. This webinar is open to anyone operating villas or short-term rentals, anywhere.
