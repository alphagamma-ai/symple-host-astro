## Stop firefighting. Start running the business.

You learned a lot at [Bali Villa Connect](https://bvrma.org/). Now it is time to implement it.

Free webinar for villa owners and small short-term rental operators, co-hosted by **Symplehost.ai × Million Dollar Host**. Let the after party continue with a practical 90-minute working session on Friday 12 June, 12:00 PM Bali Time, live on Zoom. Replay included.

## What you'll learn

1. **Bali trends and insights** — what is shaping the Bali short-term rental market in 2026 and what it means for independent operators.
2. **When you've outgrown spreadsheets** — clear signals it's time to move on, and the real cost of waiting.
3. **Simplifying operations with AI automations** — practical ways to reduce manual work across guest messaging, tasks, and daily ops.
4. **Scaling markets, branding, and marketing** — how strong operators grow beyond day-to-day firefighting.
5. **A framework to choose your system** — six questions that cut through marketing pages. Works for any PMS, not just Symplehost.
6. **Live Q&A** — bring your real-world questions to operators and platform builders who've helped villas of every size make the switch.
7. **A practical one-pager** — every registrant gets our PMS Selection Checklist.

## Who it's for

- Villa owners managing 1–10 properties independently
- Small PM companies under 30 units looking to scale
- Operators tired of WhatsApp chaos and double bookings
- Anyone curious about AI but unsure where to start
- Million Dollar Host community members and non-members — all welcome
- Short-term rental hosts anywhere — the playbook is the same

## FAQ

### Is this really free?

Yes — 100% free. Million Dollar Host and Symplehost.ai are running this as a community learning session for the villa and short-term rental industry.

### Will it be in English or Bahasa Indonesia?

Primary delivery is in English, with Bahasa support available during Q&A. Slides will be bilingual where possible.

### What if I can't attend live?

Register anyway — we'll send you the recording and the PMS selection checklist within 48 hours after the event.

### Is this a sales pitch for Symplehost.ai?

No. We'll show what a PMS looks like using Symplehost as an example, but the framework is vendor-neutral. You'll leave with a checklist you can use to evaluate any system.

### Do I need to be a Million Dollar Host member?

Not at all. This webinar is open to anyone operating villas or short-term rentals, anywhere.
