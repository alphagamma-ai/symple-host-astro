## Welcome to SympleHost

Getting started with SympleHost takes just a few minutes. This guide walks you through creating your account, adding your first property, and configuring your settings.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Visit the SympleHost signup page and enter your details. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your address.

## Step 2: Add Your First Property

Once logged in, navigate to **Properties** and click **Add Property**. Fill in the basic details including:

- Property name and type
- Location and address
- Number of bedrooms and bathrooms
- Maximum guest capacity
- Base nightly rate

## Step 3: Configure Your Settings

Head to **Settings** to customize your account preferences, notification settings, and payment configuration.
