**Coming soon: connect Beyond Pricing with SympleHost so pricing intelligence and day-to-day rental operations can work from the same source of truth.**

---

> **Status:** This integration is planned and not yet available inside SympleHost. This page explains what we are building toward and when it will be useful for hosts.

---

## What This Integration Will Help With

Beyond Pricing is used by short-term rental operators to support dynamic pricing and revenue management decisions.

The planned SympleHost integration is designed for hosts who want pricing strategy to connect cleanly with the rest of their operations:

- Listings and calendars
- Availability
- Booking channels
- Direct bookings
- Revenue visibility
- Guest and team workflows

The goal is simple: pricing should not live in a separate silo from the calendar, bookings, and operational work that depend on it.

---

## Who It Will Be For

This integration will be most useful for:

- Villa managers adjusting rates across multiple listings
- Hosts operating in seasonal markets like Bali, Phuket, Bandung, Da Nang, and other SEA destinations
- Operators using dynamic pricing to respond to demand, holidays, and booking pace
- Teams that want fewer manual pricing updates across channels
- Hosts who want pricing decisions to stay connected to actual reservation activity

If you manage one property with simple seasonal pricing, SympleHost's built-in pricing and rate rules may be enough. If you manage several properties or operate in a volatile market, a dedicated dynamic pricing tool can become more useful.

---

## Before the Integration

Without a connected pricing workflow, hosts often end up with:

- Manual rate updates across multiple systems
- Pricing changes that are not reflected consistently across channels
- Separate dashboards for pricing, bookings, and operations
- Harder reporting on whether price changes actually improved performance
- More room for mistakes during peak season or last-minute demand shifts

This is where dynamic pricing can become operationally messy: the pricing recommendation may be smart, but the workflow around it still depends on manual follow-through.

---

## After the Integration

Once available, the intended workflow is:

- Use Beyond Pricing for dynamic pricing intelligence
- Keep SympleHost as the operating system for listings, calendars, bookings, and team workflows
- Reduce manual pricing updates
- Keep pricing actions closer to real booking and availability data
- Help hosts respond faster to seasonality, demand changes, and booking pace

The integration should make revenue management feel less disconnected from daily hosting.

---

## Why This Matters for SEA Hosts

Southeast Asian short-term rental markets can move quickly.

Demand changes around:

- School holidays
- Long weekends
- Flight patterns
- Weather seasons
- Local events
- Digital nomad demand
- Regional travel trends

Static pricing leaves money on the table during high-demand periods and can keep calendars empty when demand softens. Dynamic pricing helps hosts adjust, but only if rate changes flow cleanly into the booking and operations workflow.

That is the problem this integration is intended to solve.

## Common Questions

### Is the Beyond Pricing integration live?

Not yet. It is planned, but not currently available inside SympleHost.

### Can I still manage pricing in SympleHost?

Yes. SympleHost already supports pricing setup, rate plans, and pricing calendar workflows.

### Should I use Beyond Pricing or SympleHost pricing rules?

Use SympleHost pricing rules if you need a clear manual pricing structure. A dynamic pricing integration becomes more useful when you manage multiple listings, face strong seasonality, or want pricing recommendations based on changing demand.

### Will this replace SympleHost pricing tools?

No. The goal is to connect dynamic pricing intelligence with SympleHost's existing calendar, booking, and operations workflows.
