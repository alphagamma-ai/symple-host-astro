## Overview

SympleHost integrates with WhatsApp so you can manage guest conversations from a single inbox. This guide shows you how to connect your WhatsApp Business account.

## Prerequisites

Before connecting, make sure you have:

- A WhatsApp Business account
- Admin access to your SympleHost account
- A verified phone number

## Connecting Your Account

1. Go to **Settings > Integrations > WhatsApp**
2. Click **Connect WhatsApp**
3. Follow the QR code pairing process
4. Once connected, incoming messages will appear in your unified inbox
