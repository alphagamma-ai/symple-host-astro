## What Are Tasks?

Tasks are to-do items you create and assign to your team. Whether it's a cleaning job after checkout, a maintenance fix, or a restocking run, tasks help you keep track of what needs doing, who's responsible, and when it's due.

Think of it like a shared checklist that everyone on your team can see — but each person only sees the items assigned to them.

---

## How to Create a Task

1. Click **Operations** in the sidebar to expand it.

2. Click **Tasks**.

3. Click **New Task** in the top area.

4. The **Create New Task** modal opens. Fill in:
   - **Title** (required) — a clear description of what needs to be done (placeholder: "e.g., Review guest inquiry for Villa Rosa")
   - **Description** (required) — more details about the task (placeholder: "Provide details about what needs to be done...")
   - **Assign To** (required) — select a team member from the dropdown
   - **Priority** (required) — choose **Low**, **Medium**, or **High**
   - **Due Date** (required)
   - **Start Time** (optional)
   - **Entity Type** (optional) — link the task to a Listing, Booking, Customer, Quote, or Service
   - **Tags** (optional) — add comma-separated tags like "urgent, follow-up"
   - **Job Template** (optional) — attach a pre-built checklist from your job templates

5. Click **Create Task**.

The task appears in the **Active** tab and the assigned team member can see it in their task list.

## How to Assign a Task to a Team Member

When creating a task, the **Assign To** dropdown shows all team members on your account. Select the person you want to handle the task.

💡 **Tip:** If the person you want to assign isn't in the dropdown, they may not have been added to your team yet. See: How to Add Team Members & Assign Roles.

You can also reassign a task after it's been created:

1. Click on the task to open its detail panel.
2. Use the **Assign to** dropdown (with **"Select team member"** placeholder) to change the assignee.
3. Click **Save Changes**.

## Using Job Templates for Recurring Work

For tasks you create often (like checkout cleanings), SympleHost has **Job Templates** that save you from typing the same details every time.

1. Go to **Operations** → **Jobs**.

2. You'll see job categories: **All**, **Cleaning**, **Maintenance**, **Inspection**, and **Custom**.

3. Click **Create Job** to build a new template, or use one of the **Quick start** templates:
   - **Checkout Cleaning** — "Standard turnover clean between guests"
   - **Checkout Turnover** — "Full inspection, cleaning, and restocking checklist"

4. Each job template includes:
   - **Job Name** (e.g., "Checkout Cleaning")
   - **Description**
   - **Category** dropdown
   - **Default Priority** (Low, Medium, High, or Urgent)
   - **Estimated Duration** (in minutes)
   - A **Checklist Template** with different item types:
     - **Text Item** — a text instruction
     - **Photo Required** — before photo, after photo, or before & after
     - **Count Item** — a numeric count
     - **Section Divider** — a heading to organize the checklist
     - **Yes / No** — a binary check
     - **Score (1-10)** — a rating scale
     - **Video** — a video capture
     - **Checkbox** — a simple tick-off item

When you create a task and select a Job Template, the checklist is automatically attached so the team member knows exactly what to do and can check off each item as they go.

## Task Detail Panel

Click any task to open its detail panel on the right side. You'll see:

- **Title** and **Description**
- **Due Date** and **Start Time**
- **Priority** (Low, Medium, or High)
- **Requested By** — who created the task
- **Assigned To** — who's responsible (or "Unassigned")
- **Related To** — linked listing, booking, or customer
- **Created** and **Updated** timestamps
- **Checklist** — if a job template was attached, with checkable items

Action buttons: **Edit task**, **Mark Complete**, **Archive**, and **Delete**.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't create tasks
Make sure you have access to the **Operations** section. Account Admins and Account Managers can create tasks by default. If you're a Staff member, you may only be able to view tasks assigned to you.

### My team member says they can't see the task
Check that the task is assigned to them specifically. Also confirm they have an active account — members with an **Invited** status may not be able to log in yet.

### I need to delete a task
Open the task detail panel and click **Delete**. Completed tasks remain in the **Completed** tab for your records.
